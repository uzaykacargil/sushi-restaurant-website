✦ Our Philosophy

Where Zen Meets
Culinary Mastery

Founded by Master Chef Hiroshi Tanaka, Sushi Zen was born from a lifelong dedication to the ancient traditions of Edo-style sushi. Each visit is a meditative experience — a celebration of seasonality, precision, and the quiet beauty of simplicity.


We source our fish directly from Tsukiji-certified suppliers daily, ensuring every bite is as fresh as the morning tide.
