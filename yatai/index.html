✦ Born on the Streets

REAL FOOD.
NO RULES.

YATAI started as a single ramen cart outside Shibuya station. Today we bring that same fire, those same recipes — unchanged — straight to your door. No white tablecloths. No reservations. Just bold, honest Asian street food cooked fresh every single day.

Every broth is simmered for 18+ hours. Every bao is steamed to order. Every roll is cut by hand. That's the YATAI way.
